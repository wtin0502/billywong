Hello!
I'm Wong Chun Tin Billy
a Hong Konger and an UX UI designer with unlimited enthusiasm. I am a current master graduate in Entrepreneurship and Innovation Management with distinction from Loughborough University London.  As an entrepreneurial, creative and curiosity-driven thinker, I combine eastern and western culture to develop universally palatable content and solutions.
What makes me inspired most is to communicate with people from different backgrounds. This is because I believe that communication is the most effective way to understand everything around me. That's why I love talking to people, interacting with them and questioning about something that I am unfamiliar with.
Even though I was not majoring in UX UI-related subjects, I have taken several UX UI courses online and have constantly delved into these fields during my spare time as I am loving it! I find that UX and UI are the subjects that link utility, connectivity and creativity together. They not only help businesses grow, but also more tightly connect different parties through a continued improvement and optimisation on products and services.
